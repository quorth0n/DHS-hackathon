ABOUT US

Our Story

We are four high school students currently attending Dublin High School. Because disasters can occur at any given moment, we wanted to be one step ahead. This is why we made EmUrgent, the efficient and facile way to assist those who are in urgent need while in an emergency. Our solution both includes a website (mainly for administrative use) and an app (for those who are in need and for those who need to donate).
